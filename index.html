Company Profile

Authorized Distributor for Top-Tier Global Brands.

Jiujiang Maitong Import and Export Trading Co., Ltd. is an authorized distributor of many top-tier brands, with two core business divisions: New Energy & Solar Products and Commercial Kitchen Equipment.

Purchasing from us allows clients to obtain genuine top-brand products directly. We have local subsidiaries named Green Astronaut Sdn Bhd in six Southeast Asian countries (Malaysia, Singapore, Indonesia, Thailand, Vietnam, Philippines) to handle maintenance, after-sales service, and spare parts supply.

Commercial Kitchen Equipment
