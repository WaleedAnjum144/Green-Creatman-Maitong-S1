COMPANY PROFILE

Who We Are

A trusted authorized distributor connecting customers with reliable solar products, commercial kitchen equipment, localized technical support and long-term service assurance.

English Version

Jiujiang Maitong Import and Export Trading Co., Ltd.

Jiujiang Maitong Import and Export Trading Co., Ltd. is an authorized distributor of many top-tier brands, with two core business divisions: New Energy & Solar Products and Commercial Kitchen Equipment.

Purchasing from us allows clients to obtain genuine top-brand products directly. We have local subsidiaries named Green Astronaut Sdn Bhd in six Southeast Asian countries: Malaysia, Singapore, Indonesia, Thailand, Vietnam, and the Philippines.

These local subsidiaries support maintenance, after-sales service, spare parts supply, equipment commissioning, troubleshooting and technical training.
